Welcome to The Tennis Life

Inspiring community, fitness, and fun through tennis.

Who We Are

The Tennis Life is a growing tennis community based in the UK dedicated to bringing people together through sport. Whether you're picking up a racquet for the first time or returning to the court after years, we offer a welcoming space to learn, compete, and enjoy the game.

Founded by passionate players and coaches, our mission is to make tennis more accessible and social. We host regular match days, beginner sessions, coaching clinics, and social events. We believe tennis should be fun, inclusive, and part of a healthy lifestyle.

We play at a variety of courts and collaborate with local venues to keep things flexible and engaging. Come for the sport, stay for the community.

Weekly Match Days

Join casual and competitive matches every week — players of all levels welcome!

Beginner Friendly

New to tennis? We run low-pressure sessions to help you build confidence on the court.

Community Focused

It’s not just about tennis — we build friendships, stay active, and support each other.
