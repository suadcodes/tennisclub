Welcome to The Tennis Life Club

A space for tennis lovers from East Africa and beyond — Nairobi, Kampala, Kigali, Arusha, and the diaspora.
Members enjoy access to private events, community tournaments, coaching, and cultural heritage activities.

East African Roots

Inspired by the landscapes, rhythms, and culture of the region. Our courts are places of joy and community.

Private Membership

Exclusive access for players and families. We maintain a small, respectful, and vibrant community.

Events & Tournaments

Quarterly matches, socials, and cultural evenings with local music, food, and friendly competition.
